Electric Sheep Game

A minigame where you will have to guess one of the words they show you

1.- When you start the game they will show you 3 words/phrases, you must guess which of these 3 words is the correct one, to do this they will give you a small clue, example:

GPU - Ram - Rain

2.- When you read the clue and are sure of your answer, you must write it in the indicated box. If your answer is correct you will add a point and new words will appear. If you don't get it right you won't be able to guess any more words and you will be shown the table with the correct words.
